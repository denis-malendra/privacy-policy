Privacy Policy - VisitCardWallet

Effective date: March 21, 2026

1. Overview
VisitCardWallet ("we", "our", "app") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Camera Data
Used to scan business cards
Images are processed locally on your device
Captured or imported card images may be temporarily stored locally while OCR runs and while you review extracted fields
If photo storage is disabled, the image is deleted after OCR is completed

2.2 Photos / Files
Used when user uploads an existing image of a business card
Imported images follow the same local OCR and review flow before a card is saved

2.3 Contacts
Accessed ONLY when user chooses to save a contact
We do NOT read or upload your contacts list

2.4 OCR Data (Extracted Text)
Name, phone, email, company, etc.
Stored locally on your device
OCR results may be normalized and mapped into card fields such as name, title, company, phone, email, and website
You can review and edit extracted fields before saving
Not shared unless explicitly implemented by user (e.g., export)

2.5 Analytics Data
Anonymous usage data (screens visited, crashes)
Used to improve app performance
May use third-party services (e.g., Firebase Analytics)

3. How We Use Data
Scan and digitize business cards
Allow saving contacts to device
Improve app performance and stability

4. Data Storage
All scanned card data is stored locally on your device
We do not store personal data on external servers (unless explicitly stated in future features)

5. Data Sharing
We do NOT sell or share your personal data.
Exceptions:
Legal requirements
Anonymous analytics providers

6. Permissions Justification
Camera -> scanning cards
Photos -> importing images
Contacts -> saving contacts
Internet -> analytics and updates

7. Your Rights
You can:
Delete cards anytime
Revoke permissions from device settings
Uninstall app to remove all data

8. Security
We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data.

9. Changes
We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected here.
